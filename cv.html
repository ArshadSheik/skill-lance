Professional Profile

About Me

I am an IT professional and data engineer currently pursuing Master of Information Technology at the University of Western Australia. With over two years of industry experience, I specialise in building scalable data pipelines, ETL workflows and cloud-based solutions using Python, SQL, Azure, Snowflake and AWS. I am driven by a genuine passion for turning complex data challenges into clean, efficient and maintainable systems.

My career began at Merilytics in Hyderabad, where I joined as a Database Intern and quickly built a strong foundation in relational database management, query optimisation and data integrity at scale. I progressed into a Data Engineer I role at the same firm, designing automated ingestion and transformation workflows across multiple client environments. I later joined Accordion as a Data Engineer II, where I took ownership of end-to-end ETL and ELT pipelines for financial sector clients, leveraging Python, SQL and Azure. In this role I optimised Snowflake data warehouse schemas to significantly improve reporting performance, reduced query complexity by 56% and cut execution time by 65% through advanced Python techniques including multiprocessing and multithreading. I also led the development of a Retool-based automation platform, managing the complete backend infrastructure and integrating Snowflake and AWS services to streamline operations and enhance delivery efficiency.

As a team lead at Accordion, I managed multiple workstreams simultaneously, onboarded and mentored new engineers, and consistently delivered critical client solutions on time and to a high standard. This experience sharpened both my technical depth and my ability to lead with clarity under pressure.

Since relocating to Perth, I have been contributing to the McCusker Internship Programme at Interchange WA as an IT-Data Intern. I support client and case management across Salesforce and Lumary platforms and lead data mapping and transformation tasks for a live migration to the Nightingale system. Collaborating closely with non-technical stakeholders throughout this project has strengthened my ability to communicate complex problems clearly and deliver solutions that are grounded in real organisational needs.

Alongside my data engineering career, I have developed a strong interest in web development and software engineering. At UWA, I built SkillLance entirely from scratch using HTML5, CSS3 and vanilla JavaScript, an interactive web learning platform that deepened my understanding of responsive design, accessibility, asynchronous programming and front-end architecture. This project reflects my belief that great engineering extends beyond data systems into every layer of technology.

I hold a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science Engineering from Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Technology (JNTUH), during which I published two peer-reviewed research papers in deep learning, covering retinal image analysis for diabetic retinopathy detection and real-time helmet detection for road safety. I complement this academic background with industry certifications in machine learning (Alteryx), cloud computing (NPTEL), Python automation (Google) and data science (NPTEL).

I am actively seeking graduate opportunities in data engineering, cloud infrastructure or software development across Australia. I bring a collaborative mindset, a commitment to clean and well-documented work, and a continuous drive to grow alongside the technologies and teams I work with.
