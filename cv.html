Professional Profile

I am a motivated computing student with a strong interest in front-end development, interaction design, and practical software engineering. Through university projects and personal experimentation, I have developed experience using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript to transform requirements into interactive interfaces that feel consistent across pages and screen sizes.
